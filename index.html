Know more about us

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to promote Linux as a powerful platform for creativity, productivity, and gaming. We want to support developers and users who believe in open-source software and innovation. This website exists to help people discover useful applications and promising in-development games made for Linux, while encouraging growth in the Linux community.

Provided Apps

We provide a curated selection of Linux applications focused on productivity, development, design, and everyday use. Each app is chosen for its usefulness, performance, and compatibility with Linux systems. Our goal is to help users find reliable tools that improve their workflow and show the true potential of Linux software.

Provided Games

We showcase in-development and indie games that run on Linux, giving visibility to small developers and experimental projects. These games may still be in progress, but they offer creativity, originality, and fresh ideas. By sharing these projects, we aim to support indie developers and give Linux users access to unique gaming experiences.
